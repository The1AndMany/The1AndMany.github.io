What is KAI?
Normally, KAI is the artificial intelligence that runs the day-to-day operations of Low-Rez Labs. He is also a VoiceAttack profile meant for helping people fly in Elite: Dangerous.

What does he do?
KAI's features vary as he continues to get updated, but the majority of them can be summarized as "You can control your ship by saying specific commands."

What do I need?
All you need to run KAI is a microphone (preferrably a headset), a full version of VoiceAttack, and a copy of Elite: Dangerous.

How do I install this?
Please refer to README.txt for installation instructions.

What's the difference between normal and "Lite" versions?
"Lite" versions are built with as little dependency as possible. This makes it easier to set up, but also means some features aren't included.

KAI is broken!
Sorry to hear that. If Elite: Dangerous was just updated, it's probably because I haven't been able to update to the new version yet. If that's the case, try the latest "Lite" version out and see if it still works. Otherwise, email me and I'll look into it. Please be as specific as possible, and provide steps to replicate the issue you're having. I can't respond directly, but I do read everything I get and am willing to email you from my personal email address if I need any additional information.

Still have a question? Send me an email! Unfortunately I can't respond to any emails directly, but I do read them all!
